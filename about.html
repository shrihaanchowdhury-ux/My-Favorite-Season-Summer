Why is Summer my Favorite Season?A Breakdown of My Summer

Summer is my favorite time of year because of all the happiness, excitment and adventure it brings. First off, school is over and summer break, already giving off an intense feeling of joy and anticapation for summer break activities. After all the schoolwork toil, summer provides a fresh breeze of relaxation and sense of calm and motivational energy to go outside, socialize, and be healthy. Around the first two weeks of June, my birthday rolls around, whoch always bring me happiness and childish excitment to celebrate a new age, getting gifts, doing family traditions, and throwing a memorable birthday party. Another amazing thing that comes during the summer is the community pool. Jumping, splashing swimming are always such a blast with friends and family, and it get better with pizza from Dominoes! Summer is also a time where my brother and I realize that we have a lot of free time, so we play fun games, bike around with friends, play sports outside and going to dinner parties on the weekends. The highlight of my family's summer is our annual trip to India to spend time with family and loved ones. We go through flights, 5-star hotels, long drives and breath-taking tourists attractions every day! At our grandparents house, we talk, catch up, and laugh alongside our elders, cousins and extended family, eat delicious meals together, and create life-lasting memories. Summer sometimes even brings weddings of uncles, and we attend with happy and content hearts, congratulating the bride and groom. Most importantly, a yearly trip to India helps keep our Indian culture and roots strong, and family ties unbreakable. When coming to the US, the immediate rush to buy school supplies always brings in the new school year vibe and brings positivity and inspiraton to grow and reach for the sky in the next school year.
